唐詩三百首
Dang sji sam prak sjux

#Ngox ngian kox sji

1.

Komx ngyoh

Triang Kiux-Leng

Lan jep chjyn qyoi-njyi, kueh hrua chiu keux ket. 
Hion-hion chiex srieng qih, ziih njex ye kre cet.
Zjyi trie lim se cjax, myon piung zuax siang jyet. 
Chaux muk iux puonx sim, gha giu miix njin cjet?

Krung nom iux tan kjyt, keng tuung ju lyuk lim.
Khioix qjii diih khioih nuanx, ziih iux syed ghan sim.
Khax jix cenh kra khrak, nad gha criox dryung sjim. 
Yonh miengh jyi sriox ngyoh, zsyn ghruan pyot khax zsim. 
Do ngian zjyox dau lix, chiex muk khioix myo qim?


2.

Ghrah Cjung-nom sren kua Ghuk sie sren njin siuk trih ciux

Lix Brak

Moh zyung piek sren ghrax, sren ngyat zsye njin kyoi.
Khiak koh sriox lai kengh, chang-chang ghruang chyih myoi.
Siang ghue gip den kra, dung driih khai kieng pyoi.
Lyuk triuk njip qy kengh, cheng la phyot ghrang qioi.
Huan ngian tok sriox khied, miix ciux leu gyungh hyoi.
Driang ka ngim zsyung piung, khyuk zinx gha seng hioi.
Ngax cyih kyon biuk lak, dau njen gyungh myang kioi. 


3.

Ngyat ghrax duk cjak
Lix Brak

Hrua kren qjit gho ciux, duk cjak myo siang chin.
Kiox puai qjeu mieng ngyat, tuaih qiengx zjeng sam njin.
Ngyat kioih pyot krex qimx, qiengx do zsye ngax sjin.
Zamh buanx ngyat ciang qiengx, ghrang lak syo gip chjyn.
Ngax ka ngyat buai-ghuai, ngax myox qiengx leng luanh.
Seng zji dung krau huan, cyih ghux kak pyon sanh. 
Yengx ket myo zieng ju, siang gi mruk yon hanh. 


4.

Chjyn si
Lix Brak

Qen chaux njo piek si, Zin sang te lyuk cje.
Tang kyon ghruai kyoi njit, zjex chiep duanx driang zji.
Chjyn piung pyot siang sjik, gha zrih njip la yoi?

5.

Myangh ngruk
Dox Pyox

Daih cuung byo njo gha, Ze Lox cheng myoih leux.
Zaux hruah cjyung zsjin siuh, qim jang kat huon heux.
Dangx hyung srieng zong yon, kuet zieh njip kyoi teux.
Ghuad tang ling zyet tengx, qjit lamx cjungh sren sieux.


6.

Zongh Yed pret chjox zrix
Dox Pyox

Njin srieng pyot siang kenh, dungx njo srim jox sjang.
Kim zsiek biuh gha zsiek, gyungh chiex tong cjyuk kuang.
Sjeuh criangh nong kioix zji, pjinh pyat kak jix chang.
Phyangh giuh puanh ye kyoix, kieng ho njet triung driang.
Qien trie njiih zjip caix, dryung zjangx kyon cix dang.
Siek biet kyon myoih huon, nje nriox huot zjeng ghang.
Ji njen kiengh byox cjip, myonh ngax lai gha pyang.
Myonh top myoih gip jix, khyo nje la ciux ciang.  #應爲“驅兒”，中華書局版明顯有誤
Jah yox cienx chjyn kiux, sin chjye krenh ghuang liang.
Cjyox chjing ghuad mjenh nan, qjit kiox lyex zjip sjang.
Zjip sjang jek pyot cyih, komx cix koh qih driang.
Mieng njit krek sren ngruk, sjed zrih liangx mang-mang.


7.

Kre njin

Dox Pyox

Zyet daih iux kre njin, qy kio zaix khung kuk.
Ziih yon liang kra cix, leng lak qioi chaux muk.
Kruan triung siek sang luanh, hyeng dex cau sret liuk.
Kuan kau gha cyuk luonh, pyot tok sju kuot njuk.
Sjed zieng qoh sryi hiat, myanh zrih zsye tryenx cjyuk.
Pyo seh khjeng bak nje, sin njin miix njo ngyuk.
Ghop huon zjangh trie zji, qyan-qiang pyot duk siuk.
Danx kenh sin njin sieuh, na myon giuh njin khuk?
Zaix sren zyen sjyix chieng, chjyt sren zyen sjyix druk.
Zjih bjex mreh cjyo ghuai, khen la pox mrau quk.
Trek hrua pyot chret pyat, chaix prak dungx jeng kiuk.
Then ghan chyih zsiuh bak, njit moh qiex siu triuk.


8.

Miungh Lix Brak

Dox Pyox

Siix biet jix thon sjeng, srieng biet zjang chrik chrik.
Krung nom cjangh lied diih, driuk khrak myo sieu sik.
Koh njin njip ngax miungh, mieng ngax driang siang qik.
Khyungx pyoi bieng srieng ghuon, loh yanx pyot khax chrik.
Ghuon lai piung lim cheng, ghuon pyanx kruan saih hok.
Kyon kim zaix la myangx, gha jix iux yox jik.
Lak ngyat muanx quk liang, ju ngi cjeuh ngran srik.
Sjyix sjim pua langh khuat, myo srix krau lyung tok.


9.

Miungh Lix Brak

Dox Pyox

Biu yon cjung njit ghrang, ju cix kiux pyot cjiih.
Sam jah bjin miungh kyon, zieng chin kenh kyon qih.
Kauh kyoi zjang gyuk-chyuk, khox daux lai pyot jeh.
Krung gho ta piung pua, cju cjep khyungx sjit dryih.
Chjyt muon sau brak sjux, njak biux bieng srieng cjih.
Kuan kad muanx kieng ghrua, sie njin duk zieu-zyih.
Zjuk yon myangx khuai-khuai, ciang laux sjin pyanx lyeh.
Chen chiu myanh syed mjeng, zek-mak sjin ghux zrih.


10.

Sungh Gi-Myo Ziem lak deh ghruan hiang

Yang Jyi

Sjengh daih myo qionx cjax, qieng leng zinx lai kyoi.
Zsyih liengh tung sren khrak, pyot tok koh chaix myoi.
Kioih cjiih kim muon yanx, zjuk yon ngo daux pyoi.
Krung Ghruai doh ghan zsjik, Kieng Lak byung chjyn qioi.
Trih ciux Driang-qan daux, dung sim jox ngax yoi.
Ghrang tang biu kueh drauh, myoih kioix phyot kieng pyoi.
Yanx zjyoh tad ghrang khrak, ko zjeng tang lak hyoi.
Ngo miu sjek pyot jyungh, myot yoih trie qim hioi.


11.

Sungh biet

Yang Jyi

Ghrax mrax qimx kyon ciux, myonh kyon gha sriox cji.
Kyon ngian pyot tok qih, kyoi nguah nom sren zjye.
Danx khioh mak biuh myonh, brak yon myo zinx zji.


12.

Cheng khe

Yang Jyi

Ngian njip Ghuang-hrua Chjyen, muaix driuk Cheng Khe sjyix.
Zsye sren ciang myanh tryenx, chyoh do myo prak lix.
Sjeng hyan luanh zjek triung, srik ziengx sjim zsyung lix.
Jangh-jangh phyamh ling ghrangx, dring-dring qiengh kra yoix.
Ngax sim soh jix ghren, chieng chjyen damx (?) njo chiex.
Chiengx liu buan zjek zjangx, zjye teuh ciang jix ix.


13. 

Yoih chjyen den kra

Yang Jyi

Zsia jang cjeuh khio lak, giung ghrungh ngiu jang kyoi.
Jax laux nemh miuk dung, qiex driangx ghuh kieng pyoi.
Driix kuh mrek mieu siuh, zom men sang jep hioi.
Den pyo ghax zrio cjiih, siang kenh ngiox qioi-qioi.
Cik chiex zsienh ghren jit, thriangh njen ngim sjik myoi.


14.

Se-sje yengh

Yang Jyi

Jemh srik then ghrax dryungx, Se-sje nengh kiux myoi.
Trieu ye Yat khe nriox, moh cak Ngo kiung phyoi.
Zienh njit khioix zjyo cjungh, kyoih lai pyang ngoh hioi.
Qjeu njin pyoh cjii pyonx, pyot ziih triak la qioi.
Kyon thryungx qjek kieu thaih, kyon len myo zjex pyoi.
Tang zji ghuanx sra buanx, mak tok dung chja kyoi.
Dri zsiah lin kra cix, ghrauh bjin qan khax hioi.


15.

Chiu tong Lan Sren kieh Triang Ngox

Mrangh Ghaux-Njen

Pok sren brak yon lix, qionx cjax ziih ji jyet.
Siang myangh sjih tong kau, sim zsye ngranh pyoi mjet.
Zriu qjin bak moh khix, hingh zjex chieng chiu pyat.
Zji kenh kyoi chuon njin, sra ghrang doh du hiat.
Then pen zjyoh njak zex, krung buanh cju njo ngyat.
Gha tang zaih ciux lai, gyungh cyih dryung jang cet.


16.

Ghrah njit Nom Deng ghruai Sin Dad

Mrangh Ghaux-Njen

Sren kuang huot se lak, drie ngyat ziemx tung zjangx.
Sanh pyat zsjing zsiek liang, khai hian nguah ghren chjangx.
Gha piung sungh hiang khioih, triuk loh tek chieng hiangx.
Jyuk chyox mieng gim dan, ghonh myo trie qim sjangx.
Komx chiex ghruai koh njin, cjung sieu lau miungh siangx.


17.

Siuk ngiap srii sren byang daix Teng Dad pyot cjiih

Mrangh Ghaux-Njen

Zsiek jang doh se liengx, gyon hak sjuk jix mengh.
Zsyung ngyat srieng jah liang, piung zyen muanx chieng thengh.
Zieu njin kyoi jyuk zinx, qen teux se chrio dengh.
Cji cix gi siuk lai, ko gim ghuh la kengh.


18.

Dung zyungh dex Nom Crai nguanh ngyat qik Sren-qim Chuai Sjeuh-pyox

Yang Chjang-Leng

Kau nguah nom crai zji, khai yi ngyat chrio thox.
Chieng hyoi damx sjyix muk, jenx jangh zaix chrung ghox.
Njimx-njemx kioix jeng hio, dring-dring pienh kim kox.
Miix njin chieng krung buanh, zjex jah Yat ngim khox.
Chen lix gyungh njo gha, myoi piung chjye lan dox.


19.

Zsim Se Sren qionx cjax pyot ngyoh

Khiu Ye

Zjyet tengx qjit mrau zii, drik zjangx sam zjip lix.
Khuh kruan myo dung buuk, khjye sjit jyi qanh kiix.
Njak pyoi kin zre chja, qing zjex teuh chiu sjyix.
Chrie-drie pyot siang kenh, mjinx mienx khung ngiangx cjix.
Chaux srik sin yox triung, zsyung sjeng myanx chrung lix.
Gip ci kheh qy zyet, ziih cyuk dangx sim njix.
Syi myo pjin cjyox qih, phuax tok chieng ziengh lix.
Hingh zinx pyang ghrax sren, gha pjit daix cji cix.


20.

Chjyn phyamh Njak-ja Khe

Gi-Myo Ziem

Qy qih myo duanx zyet, chiex khioh zsye sriox ngux.
Myanx piung chjye ghrang cju, hrua loh njip khe khux.
Cied jah tryenx se hak, krek sren myangh nom tux.
Dom qen pyoi jyung-jyung, lim ngyat te hiangh ghux.
Srieng zrih chiax mje muanh, ngyanh ye dri kan sux.


21.

Siuk Yang Chjang-Leng qionx kio

Zjang Kianh

Chieng khe sjim pyot chrik, qionx chjoh jyi ko yon.
Zsyung cied loh myoi ngyat, chieng kuang ju ye kyon.
Mrau deng siuk hrua qiengx, jak yenh ci dai myon.
Jo jek zsiah zji khioh, se sren luan ghak gyon.


22.

Jox Kau Sjek Siet Kioh tong Zi-qon Zsih biu-do

Zrim Srim

Thap sjed njo jyungx chjyt, ko kau syungx then kiung.
Tong lim chjyt sjed kraih, dongh daux buan hio khung.
Duot-nguot qrap zsjin cju, zreng-ghrueng njo kyoix kung.
Siih kruk ngaih brak njit, chit zong mua chang khiung.
Ghrax khjye cjiix kau teux, pyox thengh myon kieng piung.
Lien sren njak pua dau, puon cux zsix drieu tung.
Cheng ghruai krep drie daux, kiung kuanh gha leng-lung.
Chiu srik zyung se lai, chang njen muanx Kruan-triung.
Ngox-ling pok ngyan zjangx, myanh kox cheng mung-mung.
Ziengh lix leux khax ngoh, sjingh qjin siuk sriox cuung.
Zjed ciang krueh kuan khioh, kruk daux cii myo giung.


23.

Zok thuaih zsjiih kuan lih

Ngyan Ket

Kjyix Mraux sjyed, Se Ngyan zok njip Daux Cju, byon sjeu sret liak, kioi zinx nji khioh. Mieng nen, zok iuh kung Yengx-phuah Gyonh, pyot byamx chiex cju pen piix nji thuaih, khioix lik nong cjed dek jo? Kad mung gi sjang len nji jix. Cjo srih gha yeh njinx khox tring liemx, koh cak sji qjit phjen jix zsjiih kuan lih.

Siek nen byung thad bieng, sren lim njiih zjip nen.
Zyen ngyan zaix deng ghox, dungh hak tang muon zen.
Ciengx sjyed iux zjang gi, njit qranh ju tok men.
Huot njen cau sjed pienh, sruk syed chin njung cjen.
Kim lai tenx sie gyonh, sren jii iuh phyon njen.
Zjeng sieux zok pyot do, njin bin sjang khax len.
Zjex jix ghremh lin kiengx, chiex cju duk ghenh zyen.
Srih zjin ciang yang miengh, khioix pyot njo zok ien?
Kim biex tring liemx cjax, prak cji njo huax cien.
Zjyi nong zyet njin miengh, jix cak zji sjed ghen.
Si jyuk qyex byo cet, jinx kan ziih chieh zsjyen.
Ciang kra ziuh ngio mrek, kyoi laux krung gho pen.


24.

Kyonh crai yox triung jox cjo myon zrix qenh zip

Yoi Qingh-Myot

Pieng yed srim ghrueh kiek, qenh chimx nging chieng hiang.
Haix zjangx piung yox cjiih, sieu-jeu drie kak liang.
Byan qa gionx sieu sanh, kra pjin biuh muanx dang.
Ziih zam kio chjoh zriung, myoih tox sie mjin khang.
Lix ghuad zjex pyoi khjenx, siengh dat gheng ciek myang.
Sien byoi zjyuk zji kimh, srio kuax ghrengx kenh zjang.
Pyox qimx qjit puai ciux, ngiangx leng kim ngyuk cjang.
Zsjin huan thex ziih khjeng, qih jyuk ling piung zsiang.
Ngo triung zjengh myon srix, gyon ngienh kim quang jang.
Pyang trie dad pyan diih, khioix yat zai pyoh giang.


25.

Chrio pyat Jang-cix kieh Ngyan dad ghrauh sjo.

Yoi Qingh-Myot

Che-che khioh chin qaih, phyamh-phyamh njip qen myoh.
Kyoi drauh Lak-jang njin, zan cjyung Kuangx-ling zjyoh.
Kim trieu chiex ye biet, gha chjoh ghruan siang ngyoh.
Sjed zrih pua zjangx cju, jyen ghuai qan tok dryoh.


26.

Kieh Zyen-cieu sren triung daux zrix

Yoi Qingh-Myot


Kim trieu gyonh crai lrangx, huot nemh sren triung khrak.
Kranh tex sjyuk kieng sin, kyoi lai cjox brak zjek.
Jyuk dri qjit bjeu ciux, yanx qyoih piung yox zsiek.
Lak jep muanx khung sren, gha chjoh zsim ghrang ciek.


27.

Driang-qan ngyoh Biung Trioh

Yoi Qingh-Myot

Khrak zyung tung pyang lai, qioi zjangx Prah-ling yox.
Myonh khrak gha yeh lai, chaix sren qjin mrex pyox.
Meng-meng hrua cjengh khai, jang-jang qenh sin njyox.
Zak biet kim jix chjyn, pjinh si srieng kioix lyox.


28.

Zsiek chiih Hyo-ji Ghuenh

Yoi Qingh-Myot

Lak byam duh Ghruai trinh, deng pyangh lim ko jek.
Ghaux-ghaux piung khix pua, meng-meng njit drim zsiek.
Njin kyoi sren kuak qomh, ngranh ghrax lo cju brak.
Duk jah qik Zin kruan, theng cjyung myoih men khrak.


29.

Tung krau

Yoi Qingh-Myot


Lih sjah gyuk cjung nen, chjyt krau khuangh chieng zjoh.
Jang liux sanh ghua piung, cheng sren damx ngo lioh.
Qioi zung sjek ziih khied, jyen kranh ghruan biuk khioh.
Myoi yox qad phyang ngyan, chjyn kiu mieng gha chjoh.
Lak qy sim lyoh cjix, cyn zrih ciek ju gioh.
Cjung brex sie ket lio, moh Dau drik khax sjoh.


30.

Sungh Jang zjex nriox

Yoi Qingh-Myot

Yengx njit pyang chek-chek, chjyt ghrang biuh ju ju.
Nriox cix kim iux ghrang, dad krung soh khjeng cju.
Njex puaih khox myo zjix, phyox nemh qjek zi nju.
Qyh ye triangx sriox juk, liangx biet khip pyot hiu.
Tuaih chiex ket triung driang, ngieh yangx nan biuh liu.
Ziih sieux khyat nuaih hyonh, zrih ko ji ngax qiu.
Lad ci thak liengh muon, njim syt sjoh myo iu.
Bin giemx zjeng sriox zjangh, cii zyung khioix daix cju.
Ghrauh kyung cyn biux daux, jyung cjix zsjynh gi ju.
Biet lie zaix kim zjin, kenh njex tang gha chiu.
Kio ghren sjix ziih khjenx, lim komx huot nan sju.
Kyoi lai zjiih qyh nriox, leng lyih jyen qjeng liu.


31.

Zjin ngeh thrieu srii yenh duk zjen keng

Liux Cuung-Ngyan

Kip ciengx sriuh ghan chjix, chieng sim phyot drin biuk.
Ghren dri pad jep sjo, boh chjyt tung crai duk.
Cjin ngyan leux myo chyox, myangh ciek sjed sriox driuk.
Jyi ngian kiih khax meng, zjenh siengh gha ju zjuk.
Daux njin deng yox ziengx, dai srik lien sjim triuk.
Njit chjyt myoh loh jo, cheng zsyung njo kau muk.
Damx njen lie ngian sjyet, ngoh jyet sim ziih cyuk.


32.

Khe Kio

Liux Cuung-Ngyan

Kiux ye crim coq sjyuk, ghrengx chiex nom jii trek.
Ghren qioi nuung pox lin, nguh zsix sren lim khrak.
Heux kreng phyan loh chaux, jah prangh hiangx khe zjek.
Lai yangx pyot byung njin, driang ka chriox then piek.

#Ngruk pyox

33. 

Saih zjangx khyuk

Yang Chjang-Leng

Zjen mieng khung sang lim, pret ngyat seu kruan daux.
Chjyt saih njip saih ghan, chjoh chjoh ghuang lo chaux.
Zyung lai Qy Pjeng khrak, krai gyungh drin sra laux.
Mak ghruk ju ghep nje, king khrua ciex liu haux.


34.

Saih ghrax khyuk

Yang Chjang-Leng

Qimh mrax doh chiu sjyix, sjyix ghan piung zsix tau.
Bieng sra njit myoih muot, qrem-qrem kenh Lim-thau.
Siek njit driang zjeng cjenh, ghrem ngian qih khioih kau.
Ghuang drin cyuk kim kox, brak kuot luanh bung hau.


35.

Kruan sren ngyat

Lix Brak

Mieng ngyat chjyt Then Sren, chang-mang yon haix kren.
Driang piung kioix myanh lix, chjye doh Ngyuk-muon Kruan.
Hanh ghrah Brak-tong daux, gho khjye Cheng Haix qruan.
Ju lai cjeng cjenh diih, pyot kenh iux njin ghruan.
Sjyoh khrak myangh pen srik (色), si kyoi ta khox ngran.
Kau lu tang chiex jah, thanh sik myoih qing ghren.


36.

Cix jah Ngo ka

Lix Brak

Driang-qan qjit phenh ngyat, myanh ghox taux qioi sjeng.
Chiu piung chjye pyot zinx, cungx zjex ngyuk kruan zieng.
Gha njit bieng gho lox, liang njin brex yanx cjeng.


37.

Driang-kan ghrang

Lix Brak

Chiep pyat chrio phiuh ngrak, cjet hrua muon zen giek.
Lang gie triuk mrax lai, njeux zriang lungh cheng muai.
Dung kio Driang-kan lix, liangx sieux myo ghem chai.
Zjip siih ye kyon biux, siu ngran myoih zjang khai.
Te du hiangh qomh pek, chen huanh pyot qjit ghuai.
Zjip ngox sjix trienx mii, ngyanh dung drin jox huai.
Zjang zuon baux dryox sinh, khioix zjangx myangh pyo dai.
Zjip liuk kyon yanx ghrang, Gyo-dang Jemh-joh tuai.
Ngox ngyat pyot khax chjyuk, yan sjeng then zjangx qai.
Muon zen drii ghrang ciek, qjit qjit srieng lyuk dai.
Dai sjim pyot nong saux, lak jep chiu piung caux.
Pret ngyat gho dep ghuang, srung pyoi se yan chaux.
Komx chiex sjang chiep sim, zuax zriu ghung ngran laux.
Caux myanx ghrah Sam-Pra, joh ciang sjo pauh kra.
Siang ngieng pyot daux yanx, drik cjiih Driang-piung Sra.


38. 

Liet nriox chauh

Mrangh Krau

Ngo dung siang daix laux, qyan-qiang ghuad srung siix.
Trieng biux kyoih zsynh (殉) pyo, sjax srieng jek njo chiex.
Pua lan zjed pyot khix, chiep sim kox ciengx sjyix.


39. 

Ju cix ngim

Mrangh Krau

Zi mux sjux triung sienh, ju cix sjin zjangx qioi.
Lim ghrang mit mit byung, qih khyungx drii drii kyoi.
Zjyi ngian chuonh chaux sim, pauh tok sam chjyn hyoi.

Ngyuk ngran pyot gip ghan qra srik, ju tad cjeu jang njit qiengx lai.


300. 

Chjyt saih

Yang Chjang-Leng

Zin zji mieng ngyat Hanh zji kruan, myanh lix driang cjeng njin myoih ghruan.
Danx srix Lyung zjeng pyoi ciangh zaix, pyot krauh gho mrax doh Qim sren.


301. 

Chieng bieng deuh

Lix Brak

Yon siangx qioi zjang hrua siangx jyung, chjyn piung phyot ghramx loh ghrua nryung.
Njak pyo gyon ngyuk sren du kenh, ghuad hiangh jeu dai ngyat ghrax byung.
Qjit cje ghung jemh loh nging hiang, yon yox Myo sren qyangx duanx driang.
Ciah myonh Hanh kiung zjyi tok zsix, khax len pyoi qenh qiex sin criang.
Mjeng hrua khjyeng kuok liang siang huan, zjang tok kyon yang tad sieuh khan.
Krex sjek chjyn piung myo ghrenx ghonh, drim hiang deng pok qiex lan kan.


302. 

Chjyt saih

Yang Cji-Huanh

Khiang dek gha syo qyanh jang liux, chjyn piung pyot doh ngyuk muon kruan.


303. 

Kim lyox qioi

Dox Chiu-Nriang
